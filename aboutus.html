ABOUT US !!!


At HealthBridge, we are dedicated to helping elderly individuals and people experiencing homelessness access the health coverage they deserve. Navigating health insurance can be overwhelming, especially for those facing life’s toughest challenges. That’s why we provide free, compassionate support to guide you through the sign-up process—ensuring no one is left behind when it comes to healthcare.



Haniel Kebede, CEO:
Kebede, the visionary behind HealthBridge, is dedicated to making healthcare more accessible and affordable for everyone. With a passion for innovation and a deep commitment to patient advocacy, she founded HealthBridge to bridge the gap between individuals and quality healthcare services. Under her leadership, the platform has become a trusted resource, empowering users to find the care they need without financial or logistical barriers.


Lisa Bailey, COO:
As Chief Operating Officer of HealthBridge, Bailey is committed to streamlining access to affordable healthcare. With a background in operations and a passion for healthcare equity, she ensures that our platform runs efficiently, connecting users with the care they need. Bailey leads with innovation and integrity, driving our mission to make healthcare more accessible for all.


Dr. Robert Johnson, CFO:
As Chief Financial Officer of HealthBridge, Dr. Johnson is dedicated to ensuring financial sustainability while keeping healthcare access affordable for all. With a strong background in financial strategy and a passion for social impact, he oversees resources to maximize efficiency and growth. Johnson plays a key role in driving our mission forward, ensuring that our platform remains a reliable and cost-effective solution for those seeking quality healthcare.


Dr. Tanya Brooks, CIO:
As Chief Information Officer of HealthBridge, Dr. Brooks leads the technological innovation that powers our platform. With expertise in data security, system optimization, and user experience, he ensures that finding affordable and accessible healthcare is seamless and secure. Dr. Brooks is passionate about leveraging technology to break down barriers, making healthcare navigation easier for all.
