Mission Statement:


Our mission is to empower individuals and families by simplifying access to health insurance coverage. Our Core Values:


clarity: We provide clear guidance through the process of matching eligibility


accessibility: This website is completely free and can be accessed on any device


equity: Our entire service is centered around breaking healthcare barriers in underserved communities by allowing them access to the coverage they need


privacy: Much like doctor-patient confidentiality, we ensure that your user data and private info is handled with the utmost care and confidentiality


Our Target Audience: anyone who is confused about or needs support finding out what health insurance they're eligible for: older people, people of low socioeconomic status, and especially homeless individuals.
